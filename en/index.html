Who Are We?

In Troublemakers We believe that fun has no limits. We are a creative team passionate about designing unique experiences that bring people together through games full of excitement, laughter, and a touch of irreverence. Our flagship project, SapoPerro, was born from the idea of transforming any gathering into an unforgettable moment, blending strategy, competition, and a relaxed atmosphere for all types of players.

We work with dedication on every detail: from the design of the cards and rules, to the quality of the materials and final presentation. We don’t just create a game, we create an experience to share with friends, family, and anyone daring enough to take on the challenge. Because at Troublemakers, it’s not just about playing, it’s about living it.
