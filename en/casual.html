For those who like to hang out.

Before playing:

  • Deal 3 cards to each player.
  • Place 3 face-up cards and 3 face-down cards in front of each player.
  • The rest of the deck stays in the center of the table.
  • Players may swap cards from their hand with the face-up ones before starting.

Game start:

  • The player with the lowest card starts.

Turns:

  • On each turn, place a card following right-to-left order.
  • Always keep 3 cards in your hand; draw if you have fewer.
  • The card played must be equal to or higher than the previous one.
  • If you can't play, take all the cards from the pile.
  • When the deck runs out, use your face-up cards first, then the face-down ones.
  • The first to run out of cards wins.

Special Cards:

  • Fire: Removes all cards from the table and the next player starts.
  • Lower: The next player must play an equal or lower number.
  • Toad: The next player must play a purple card.
  • Dog: The next player must play an orange card.
  • Mirror: Copies the previously played card.

Note: If multiple powers are played at the same time, the last card played takes effect.
