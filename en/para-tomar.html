For those who see life as a joke.

In this mode, play however you like, including the following:

  • Frog: If someone looks at you while drawing, they must drink with you for being a frog.
  • Dog: You choose someone to be your dog; every time you drink, they must drink too.
  • Lower: Everyone shows their highest card; the player with the lowest must drink. Powers count as 10. If no cards are in hand, it counts as 1.
  • Fire: You set a rule that everyone must follow until the next fire.
  • Mirror: That person chooses a game, and the loser drinks.
